# Restaurant Information

## Basic Details
- **Name**: Le Café du Marché
- **Owners**: Paul and Matthias
- **Type**: French brasserie / bistro, traditional, homemade cuisine
- **Established**: 1946 (historic Nantes institution)
- **Location**: Nantes, near La Cité des Congrès
- **Instagram**: @cafe_du_marche_nantes
- **Website (old)**: lecafedumarche.com

## Pricing
- **Average meal**: ~26€
- **Formule midi** (Entrée + Plat or Plat + Dessert): 16,50€
- **Formule complète** (Entrée + Plat + Dessert): 20€
- **Evening à la carte**: from 18€

## Signature Dishes & Weekly Specials
- **Terrine du chef** — famous, served at the table in a clay pot (unlimited, self-serve)
- **Tête de veau** — Thursday & Friday lunch (a house specialty)
- **Kig ha farz** — Tuesday (Breton stew)
- **Couscous** — Wednesday

## Menu Philosophy
- Everything homemade ("fait maison")
- Daily changing menu based on market produce
- Generous portions — the terrine pot at the table is iconic
- Classic French brasserie dishes with care and quality ingredients
- Wine selection that changes frequently

## Atmosphere
- Varnished wood interiors
- Olive green tones
- Warm, convivial, neighborhood feel
- Near the convention center — gets business crowds at lunch
- Friendly, personal service — they know their regulars
- Often described as "comme à la maison" (like home)

## Nantes Identity
- Tour Lu — iconic Nantes landmark, beloved by the owners
- "Marché" in the name comes from the old MIN (Marché d'Intérêt National) — NOT Marché de Talensac
- Cité des Congrès neighborhood
- Proud to be Nantais
- Institution since 1946 — part of the city's fabric
